Our Story

Cake, Confetti and Fun began with one mum and one big idea — to take the pressure off parents and put the fun back into parties. After years of juggling suppliers, sorting snacks, and clearing up balloons, she saw a gap: no one was taking care of everything. So we built a team of kind, capable people with all the right skills — from decoration and entertainment to food and tidy-ups. We’re here to create memorable, joyful celebrations for kids, while giving parents back their time (and their sanity).

Why Choose Us

Planning a party can be overwhelming — but it doesn’t have to be. We take care of every detail, from balloons to backup plans, so grown-ups can relax and enjoy the day. You bring the guests, we bring the magic (and glitter).

Our Mission

Childhood memories are shaped by moments of joy, and we’re here to create them. Our mission is to deliver parties full of colour, creativity, and laughter — the kind of days that children remember for years to come.
